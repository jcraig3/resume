About

Hi, I am Jayden Craig! I am a graduate of Loyola Marymount University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. My background spans various areas of technology, from web development to IT support, quality assurance, and data analysis.

In web development, I've worked on projects like MetroTune, a music-themed web app built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Figma, as well as API integrations like Pokemon-API. I focus on creating seamless user experiences by combining technical expertise with thoughtful design.

In IT, I have hands-on experience providing technical support, troubleshooting issues, and assisting users in dynamic environments. I’ve developed the ability to quickly solve problems, maintain systems, and communicate effectively to support team members and clients.

My quality assurance experience includes testing software for reliability and usability. I’ve written test cases, identified bugs, and helped ensure smooth development cycles by maintaining high-quality standards.

With data analysis, I’ve utilized tools like Python and R to conduct statistical analysis and visualizations. I’ve worked on projects that explore probability distributions and random variables, which enhanced my ability to extract insights and present findings clearly.

Outside of tech, I enjoy restoring my 1969 El Camino, audio engineering, and staying active through fitness and the outdoors. My diverse skill set allows me to bring a well-rounded, solutions-driven approach to every project I work on.

Enthusiastic, communicative, and goal-oriented, I bring a diverse skill set to any role.
